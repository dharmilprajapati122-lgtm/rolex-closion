Quiet precision

Built around restraint, balance, and mechanical depth.

The Perpetual pairs a polished 39 mm case with a five-link bracelet, a curved sapphire crystal, and a dial finished in layered green lacquer. Every surface is designed to catch light without shouting for it.

Visible complexity, tuned for daily wear.

The automatic movement carries a 68-hour reserve, a free-sprung balance, and a decorated rotor visible through the exhibition case back. It is regulated in six positions before casing.
